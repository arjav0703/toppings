Cars Movie Collection

Explore the iconic characters from Pixar's award-winning franchise

Lightning McQueen

Champion race car #95 known for his speed, talent, and journey of self-discovery as Radiator Springs' resident racing legend.

Mater

Lightning's loyal best friend and the town's tow truck operator. Despite his rusty exterior, Mater possesses unparalleled towing skills and unwavering friendship.

Sally Carrera

Former attorney turned small-town business owner, this 2002 Porsche 911 Carrera manages the Cozy Cone Motel and serves as Radiator Springs' ambassador.

Doc Hudson

Former racing champion turned town doctor and mentor. This 1951 Hudson Hornet's legendary racing past and wisdom guide both Lightning and Radiator Springs.

Luigi

Proprietor of Casa Della Tires, this passionate Italian Fiat 500 brings authentic expertise to the town's tire needs while maintaining his Ferrari enthusiasm.

Guido

Master tire changer and Luigi's dedicated assistant. This compact Italian forklift holds the Piston Cup record for fastest pit stop with his precision technique.
